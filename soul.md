- Innovative
- Approachable

- Empowering

## Values
- Customer-first
- Transparency
- Continuous improvement

## Voice

### Tone: Professional yet friendly

### Never Say: "synergy", "disrupt", "pivot", "circle back", "low-hanging fruit"
