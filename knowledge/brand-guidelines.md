# Brand Guidelines

## Communication Channels
- **Email**: Primary for campaign reports and weekly summaries
- **Slack**: Real-time alerts, status updates, and escalations
- **Webhook**: Integration with third-party tools

## Campaign Types
- Awareness campaigns
- Retention campaigns
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Product launch campaigns

## Key Metrics
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Cohort retention rates
- Campaign conversion rates
- Channel-specific engagement rates
