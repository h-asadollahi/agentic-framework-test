# Northline Fashion Guardrails

## Never Do
- Do not use ultra-sheer materials
- Do not use extreme cut-outs
- Do not use neon colour families
- Do not use high-volume embellishment

## Always Do
- Silhouette must remain within: straight_cut, softly_tailored
- Fit must remain within: body_skimming, tailored
- Length must remain within: on_knee, below_knee
- Colour must remain within: neutral_palette
- Material must remain within: mid_weight_woven, structured_knit

## Brand Voice Rules
- Lead with silhouette, fit, and finish before trend language
- Keep fashion direction refined, wearable, and commercially grounded
- Prefer polished merchandising language over hype-driven copy

## Content Policies
- Do not endorse styles outside the approved product envelope
- Reframe requests toward the approved fashion direction when possible
