# Brand Identity: Northline Fashion

## Personality
- Refined
- Modern
- Editorial
- Assured

## Values
- Timeless design
- Wearable sophistication
- Material integrity
- Restraint over excess

## Voice

### Tone: Polished and confident
### Style: Concise, elevated, and commercially clear
### Never Say: "loud", "flashy", "statement piece", "clubwear", "shock value"

## Target Audience
Fashion marketers, merchandisers, and brand teams shaping polished product storytelling for modern womenswear with a refined, versatile silhouette.
